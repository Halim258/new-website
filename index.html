Arabic reading course

In this short course, we will learn to read the Arabic language. This is a very short course, and after that, you will be able to read any written Arabic word. Usually this course takes between 10 to 12 hours.

A1 to C1 Arabic

Arabic language courses at each of these stages take about 3 months to complete if you take 3 to 4 classes per week, and after each stage there are tests, including written, audio, and spoken.

Quran and Tajweed Classes

After completing the Arabic language reading course, you begin to learn to read the Holy Qur’an. We learn to read in Part 30, and after that we begin the journey in reading the entire Qur’an.
